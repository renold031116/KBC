About Us

Welcome to Knotts BBQ Co. At Knotts BBQ Co., we turn every meal into a mouth-watering experience. Nestled in the heart of BBQ country, our passion for authentic, slow-cooked barbecue shines through in every bite. Whether you're a seasoned pitmaster or a grilling novice, we provide you with the knowledge, tools, and recipes to master the art of BBQ. Our story began in 1995, fueled by a love for smoky flavors and a desire to share the joy of perfectly cooked meat with the world. From our tender smoked brisket to our juicy pulled pork, each dish is crafted with care and expertise, using only the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques. Join us at Knotts BBQ Co. to discover a community of BBQ enthusiasts who value quality, flavor, and tradition. Explore our step-by-step tutorials, delicious recipes, and expert tips that make mastering BBQ easy and fun. Whether you're firing up the grill for a family gathering or perfecting your technique, we're here to help you every step of the way.
